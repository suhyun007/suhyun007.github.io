Privacy Policy

HaruFit App Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The HaruFit app processes personal information to provide diet management services and improve user experience. If the purpose changes, we will take necessary measures, such as obtaining additional consent, in accordance with applicable laws.

  • Provide weight tracking and meal logging services
  • Deliver personalized diet guidance and AI-powered consulting
  • Generate progress charts and statistics
  • Send push notifications for diet reminders
  • Improve app functions and services

2. Collection and Processing of Personal Information

Information collected and stored on servers:

  • User profile: Nickname, age, gender, height, target weight, current weight, daily calorie goal, diet start date, profile image
  • Weight records: Daily weight measurements and dates
  • Meal records: Meals logged (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks) with dates
  • Device information: Device token for push notifications
Data storage:
  • User data is stored securely on Supabase servers (cloud database) to enable synchronization across devices
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Data is used solely for providing the service and improving the app experience

3. Retention and Deletion of Personal Information

User data is retained on our servers as long as the account is active. Users can request data deletion at any time through the app settings or by contacting support.

Data retention policy:
  • Data is retained while the app is in use
  • When a user requests account deletion, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Device tokens are immediately deleted when push notifications are disabled
  • If the app is uninstalled without account deletion, data remains on servers but is inaccessible

4. Provision to Third Parties

HaruFit does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following cases:

Third-party services:
  • Supabase: Cloud database service for secure data storage
  • Push notification services: Device tokens shared only for delivering notifications
  • AI service providers: When using AI consulting features, your diet-related information may be processed by AI service providers to generate personalized advice
  • All third-party services are required to comply with data protection regulations

5. Protection of Personal Information

We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security measures:
  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure cloud infrastructure provided by Supabase
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • User data is never shared without explicit consent

6. User Rights

Users have the following rights regarding their personal information:

User rights:
  • Right to access: View all stored personal information
  • Right to correction: Update or correct personal information through app settings
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of account and all associated data
  • Right to opt-out: Disable push notifications at any time
  • Right to data portability: Request export of your data

7. Changes to this Policy

This policy may be updated due to legal, policy, or technical changes. Users will be notified via app updates or announcements. Continued use of the app after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Change history:
  • January 1, 2025: Initial implementation
