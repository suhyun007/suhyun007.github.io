Privacy Policy

Saju App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Purpose of Personal Information Processing

Saju App processes personal information for the following purposes. If the purpose changes, we will take necessary measures such as obtaining separate consent in accordance with Article 18 of the Personal Information Protection Act.

1.1 Service Provision

  • Provision of Saju fortune-telling services
  • Provision of personalized content
  • App functionality and service improvement

1.2 Customer Support

  • Response and processing of inquiries
  • Guidance on service usage
  • Complaint handling and dispute resolution

2. Personal Information Collected

Required Information:
  • Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Time of Birth (Optional)
  • Place of Birth

Optional Information:

  • User status information (relationship status, marriage, etc.)
  • App usage statistics data

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (OS version, device model)
  • App usage logs
  • Push notification tokens

3. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

Saju App destroys personal information without delay once the purpose of collection and use has been achieved. However, if it is necessary to retain information in accordance with relevant laws and regulations, we will retain user information for the period specified in the relevant laws and regulations.

3.1 Retention Basis and Period

  • Records on contracts or withdrawal of subscription: 5 years (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce)
  • Records on payment and supply of goods: 5 years (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce)
  • Records on consumer complaints or dispute resolution: 3 years (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce)
  • Website visit records: 3 months (Communications Secret Protection Act)

4. Third-Party Provision of Personal Information

Saju App processes personal information only within the scope specified in Article 1 (Purpose of Personal Information Processing) and does not process beyond the original scope or provide to third parties without prior consent.

However, personal information may be processed in the following cases:
  • When the user has given prior consent to third-party provision
  • When required by law or for investigation purposes in accordance with procedures and methods established by law
  • When the user or legal representative is unable to express intention or when prior consent cannot be obtained due to unknown address, etc., and it is clearly necessary for the urgent life, body, or property interests of the user or third party

5. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

Saju App outsources personal information processing tasks as follows for smooth personal information operations.

5.1 Outsourcing Companies and Tasks

  • Cloud Service Providers: Server hosting and data storage
  • Analytics Service Providers: App usage statistics analysis
  • Push Notification Services: Push notification delivery

When concluding outsourcing contracts, we specify in documents such as contracts matters related to prohibition of personal information processing beyond the purpose of outsourced tasks, technical and administrative protection measures, restrictions on re-outsourcing, management and supervision of outsourced companies, and liability for damages in accordance with Article 26 of the Personal Information Protection Act, and supervise whether outsourced companies safely process personal information.

6. User Rights and Obligations

6.1 User Rights

  • Request for access to personal information
  • Request for correction in case of errors
  • Request for deletion
  • Request for suspension of processing

6.2 User Obligations

  • Provide accurate personal information
  • Immediately modify when personal information changes
  • Prohibit unauthorized collection, use, and disclosure of others' personal information

7. Security Measures for Personal Information

Saju App takes the following security measures in accordance with Article 29 of the Personal Information Protection Act.

7.1 Administrative Measures

  • Restriction of access to personal information
  • Minimization and education of employees handling personal information
  • Operation of dedicated personal information protection organization

7.2 Technical Measures

  • Encryption of personal information
  • Technical countermeasures against hacking
  • Storage and tampering prevention of access records to personal information

7.3 Physical Measures

  • Security of physical storage locations for personal information
  • Security of computers and other electronic devices handling personal information
