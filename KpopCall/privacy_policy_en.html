KpopCall - Your Trust, Our Commitment

KpopCall ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require users to create an account to access any features. Optional login is available via Apple or Google accounts, which may provide us with the following information only if you choose to log in:

  • Apple ID or Google account identifier (for login purposes only)
  • Notification preferences

No other personal data, such as messages, contacts, or location, is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through optional login is used only to:

  • Enable personalized notifications
  • Back up notification preferences for account continuity

We do not sell, share, or use this information for advertising purposes.

3. Data Retention

Your optional login data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the personalized features you opted into. Non-logged-in users have no personal data stored.

4. Your Rights

You may choose to delete your optional login account at any time. After deletion, your personal information will be removed from our systems.
