Privacy Policy

LunaVerse App Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The LunaVerse app processes personal information solely to provide and improve services. If the purpose changes, we will take necessary measures, such as obtaining additional consent, in accordance with applicable laws.

  • Provide literary content such as episodes, poetry readings, and daily guides
  • Deliver personalized content
  • Improve app functions and services

2. Collection and Processing of Personal Information

No personal information is collected:
  • The app may request date of birth, gender, or birthplace, but this data is never stored on servers or transmitted externally.
  • Entered information is used only within the app for personalized services (e.g., episodes, poetry readings, guides).
  • Only the device token for push notifications is stored; no other personal information is collected.

Local storage:

  • User input (e.g., birth info) is temporarily stored only on the device and deleted when the app is uninstalled.
  • Device tokens for push notifications are deleted immediately when the user disables notifications or removes the app.

3. Retention and Deletion of Personal Information

This app does not store personal information (date of birth) on servers or externally, but temporarily stores it only on the device. Data is retained only on the device until the app is deleted, and all data is immediately deleted when the app is uninstalled. Device tokens for push notifications are immediately deleted when the user disables notifications or removes the app.

Data retention policy:
  • No personal information stored on servers
  • Minimize external leakage by processing only within the device
  • All data immediately deleted when app is uninstalled

4. Provision to Third Parties

This app does not provide any personal information to third parties.

No third-party provision:
  • No personal information stored on servers, making third-party provision impossible
  • All data processed only within the device
  • No transmission to external servers or databases

5. Protection of Personal Information

This app does not store personal information on servers and processes it only within the device to minimize external leakage.

Security measures:
  • No personal information stored on servers
  • Minimize external leakage by processing only within the device
  • All data immediately deleted when app is uninstalled

6. User Rights

Users may opt out of push notifications at any time through app settings. Providing birth information is optional and not required to use the app.

User rights:
  • Right to opt out of push notifications
  • Right to choose whether to provide birth information
  • Right to delete all data by uninstalling the app

7. Changes to this Policy

This policy may be updated due to legal, policy, or technical changes. Users will be notified via app updates or announcements.

Change history:
  • September 1, 2025: Initial implementation
  • September 1, 2025: Policy change to not collect personal information
